# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15th Decmber 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Easy Sanskrit, ("we," "our," or "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect

Our application collects the following information:

- Facebook Page access information
- Instagram Business account access information


## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

- Posting content to your Facebook Page and Instagram Business account

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously:

- We do not store your content permanently
- Access tokens are securely handled
- We use industry-standard security measures
- We do not share your data with third parties

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Revoke access to your Facebook and Instagram accounts
- Request information about your data usage

## 6. Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data:

- Email us
- Remove our app's access through your Facebook settings
- All data will be deleted within 30 days of request

## 7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please visit privacy policy on our website.
